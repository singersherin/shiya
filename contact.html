Sherin Shiya

is not just a singer — she is a voice that echoes through hearts. Hailing from the serene town of Angadipuram in Malappuram, Kerala, her music blends the soul of tradition with the passion of emotion. From the timeless rhythms of Mappila Pattukal to the spiritual depth of Sufi songs and the charm of modern album tracks, Sherin's versatility knows no bounds. With countless live stage performances across Kerala, she has become a beloved presence in the music community. Her voice carries warmth, sincerity, and a divine connection that moves listeners. Whether in a quiet melody or a powerful chorus, Sherin Shiya brings stories to life through her songs. Today, she stands not just as a performer, but as an artist who lives and breathes music — a rising star shining with authenticity and heart
